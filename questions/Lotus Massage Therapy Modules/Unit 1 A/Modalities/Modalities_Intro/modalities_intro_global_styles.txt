1. Q: Which massage style involves acupressure and assisted stretching along energy lines?
   A. Swedish
   B. Thai
   C. Rolfing
   D. Reflexology
   Correct Answer: Thai

2. Q: Which massage system uses long, flowing strokes and is rooted in Hawaiian tradition?
   A. Shiatsu
   B. Lomi Lomi
   C. Tui-na
   D. Ayurvedic
   Correct Answer: Lomi Lomi

3. Q: Which global style of massage is primarily focused on applying pressure to reflex zones on the feet and hands?
   A. Shiatsu
   B. Reflexology
   C. Rolfing
   D. Swedish
   Correct Answer: Reflexology

4. Q: Which massage style is based on Traditional Chinese Medicine and focuses on pushing, pulling, and acupressure techniques?
   A. Tui-na
   B. Ayurvedic
   C. Shiatsu
   D. Lomi Lomi
   Correct Answer: Tui-na

5. Q: Which Japanese massage technique uses finger pressure along meridian points to balance energy?
   A. Amma
   B. Reflexology
   C. Shiatsu
   D. Myofascial Release
   Correct Answer: Shiatsu

6. Q: Which massage style is deeply intertwined with Indian health philosophy and uses warm oils and marma point therapy?
   A. Thai
   B. Ayurvedic
   C. Tui-na
   D. Shiatsu
   Correct Answer: Ayurvedic

7. Q: Which technique is widely taught in Western schools and emphasizes techniques like effleurage and petrissage?
   A. Swedish Massage
   B. Shiatsu
   C. Lomi Lomi
   D. Tui-na
   Correct Answer: Swedish Massage

8. Q: Which massage modality is structured as a 10-session series designed to align the body with gravity?
   A. Rolfing
   B. Ayurvedic
   C. Thai
   D. Shiatsu
   Correct Answer: Rolfing

9. Q: Which massage style traditionally includes movement meditation called 'mentastics'?
   A. Swedish
   B. Trager Method
   C. Shiatsu
   D. Reflexology
   Correct Answer: Trager Method

10. Q: Which massage systems primarily follow the concept of energy channels or meridians?
   A. Swedish and Rolfing
   B. Shiatsu and Tui-na
   C. Ayurvedic and Reflexology
   D. Thai and Swedish
   Correct Answer: Shiatsu and Tui-na
