1. Q: Which hormone regulates metabolism and is produced by the thyroid gland?
   A. Insulin
   B. Thyroxine (T4)
   C. Cortisol
   D. Testosterone
   Correct Answer: Thyroxine (T4)

2. Q: Which hormone helps regulate blood sugar by promoting glucose uptake into cells?
   A. Adrenaline
   B. Glucagon
   C. Insulin
   D. Growth Hormone
   Correct Answer: Insulin

3. Q: Which hormone is released in response to stress and is produced by the adrenal cortex?
   A. Epinephrine
   B. Cortisol
   C. Glucagon
   D. Aldosterone
   Correct Answer: Cortisol

4. Q: What hormone is responsible for the 'fight or flight' response?
   A. Cortisol
   B. Insulin
   C. Epinephrine (Adrenaline)
   D. Melatonin
   Correct Answer: Epinephrine (Adrenaline)

5. Q: Which hormone is released by the pancreas to increase blood glucose levels?
   A. Insulin
   B. Thyroxine
   C. Glucagon
   D. Cortisol
   Correct Answer: Glucagon

6. Q: Which hormone promotes water reabsorption in the kidneys?
   A. Aldosterone
   B. Cortisol
   C. Antidiuretic hormone (ADH)
   D. Calcitonin
   Correct Answer: Antidiuretic hormone (ADH)

7. Q: Which hormone regulates the sleep-wake cycle?
   A. Serotonin
   B. Cortisol
   C. Melatonin
   D. Dopamine
   Correct Answer: Melatonin

8. Q: Which hormone is involved in milk ejection during breastfeeding?
   A. Prolactin
   B. Estrogen
   C. Oxytocin
   D. LH
   Correct Answer: Oxytocin

9. Q: What hormone stimulates growth and cell reproduction?
   A. Insulin
   B. Growth Hormone
   C. Cortisol
   D. ADH
   Correct Answer: Growth Hormone

10. Q: Which hormone is primarily responsible for the development of male secondary sex characteristics?
   A. Estrogen
   B. Progesterone
   C. Testosterone
   D. LH
   Correct Answer: Testosterone
