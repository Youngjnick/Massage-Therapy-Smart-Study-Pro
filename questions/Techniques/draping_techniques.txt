1. Q: What is the primary purpose of draping in massage therapy?
   A. To decorate the massage table
   B. To control temperature
   C. To protect client privacy and maintain professional boundaries
   D. To apply pressure to certain areas
   Correct Answer: To protect client privacy and maintain professional boundaries

2. Q: Which areas must always remain draped unless being treated?

   C. Chest, pelvis, and gluteal region
   D. Feet and toes
   Correct Answer: Chest, pelvis, and gluteal region

3. Q: What should a therapist do if the drape shifts and reveals a private area?
   A. Ignore it and continue

   C. Stop and immediately re-cover the area

   Correct Answer: Stop and immediately re-cover the area
